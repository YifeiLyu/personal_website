About me

I’m a Robotics Software Engineer with a strong foundation in AI, control systems, motion planning, and computer vision.

I hold a Master’s degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (EECS) from UC Berkeley and a Bachelor's in Robotics Engineering from Southeast University.

My work spans across quadruped robots, humanoid robots, warehouse AGVs, AMRs, and collaborative robots, with hands-on experience in both industry and academic research.

I’m passionate about building intelligent robotic systems that operate reliably in dynamic real-world environments.
